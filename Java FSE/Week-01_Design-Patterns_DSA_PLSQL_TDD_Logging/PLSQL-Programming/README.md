## Exercises Completed

### Exercise 1 – Control Structures

#### Scenario 1
Apply a 1% discount to the loan interest rate for customers above 60 years of age.

#### Scenario 2
Promote customers with a balance greater than 10,000 to VIP status.

#### Scenario 3
Display reminders for loans that are due within the next 30 days.

---

### Exercise 3 – Stored Procedures

#### Scenario 1
Process monthly interest for all savings accounts by applying 1% interest.

#### Scenario 2
Update employee salaries by applying a bonus percentage to a specified department.

#### Scenario 3
Transfer funds between two accounts after validating sufficient balance.
